How It Works
  1. Upload your existing resume (PDF/DOCX)
  2. Parser extracts text, paragraphs, and heading candidates
  3. Analyzer detects sections, order, and content styles
  4. Theme generator creates a JSON Resume theme matching your layout
  5. Your tailored resume is rendered with the new theme
